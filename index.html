WebGL hero — how it works

The hero canvas is a real-time WebGL2 raymarcher. The fragment shader traces rays through a Signed Distance Field (SDF), lighting the hit point with diffuse + specular + rim lighting, then applies depth fog.

Accessibility: if prefers-reduced-motion is enabled, we auto-pause. Performance: DPR is capped (≤2) and the Quality toggle adjusts step count.
