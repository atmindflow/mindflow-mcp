What’s new in 1.5.1 (summary)

High-level themes—every item below is new or improved specifically in 1.5.1.

  • Navigation: a new liquid bottom navigation bar for a more fluid experience.
  • Favorites & Feedback: quick actions now live on the app details screen.
  • Reliability & Sync: smarter data migration + improved system sync (notably on Android).
  • Localization: added Korean, Bengali, Italian + translation quality and tooling fixes.
  • UI/UX: Home Composable refactor, search improvements, font/theme cleanup, and overall polish.
  • Authentication & Stability: auth improvements and an Android update bug fix.

Detailed changes

Expandable notes with concise, user-focused impact.

NavigationNEW in 1.5.1 ⌄

Liquid bottom bar

  • Introduces a liquid bottom navigation bar for smoother motion and clearer tab focus.
  • Designed to feel more responsive without adding heavy UI overhead.
Favorites & FeedbackNEW/IMPROVED in 1.5.1 ⌄
  • New Favorites action on the app details screen for faster saving.
  • New Feedback action on the app details screen to reduce friction when reporting issues.
Reliability & SyncIMPROVED in 1.5.1 ⌄
  • Smarter data migration layer to reduce edge-case failures across upgrades.
  • Improved system sync behavior—especially tuned for Android environments.

UI/UX, theming & platform

Refinement work that lands in 1.5.1.

LocalizationNEW/IMPROVED in 1.5.1 ⌄
  • Added languages: Korean, Bengali, Italian.
  • Improved translations, localized READMEs and theme options.
  • Fixes: apostrophe escaping, consistent Japanese translation filename.
  • Thanks to community contributors for translation improvements.
UI/UX & ThemingIMPROVED in 1.5.1 ⌄
  • Home screen Composable refactored into smaller components (cleaner structure, easier iteration).
  • Search experience improvements for quicker discovery.
  • Font resources and theme cleanup to reduce redundancy and improve consistency.
  • General UI/UX polish across the app—spacing, states, and micro-interactions.
Authentication & Android stabilityIMPROVED in 1.5.1 ⌄
  • Authentication improvements for a smoother sign-in experience.
  • Android update bug fix to reduce upgrade-related failures.
