WebGL Scene: Technical Notes

The hero canvas renders a real-time 3D scene using WebGL2 and GLSL raymarching. The fragment shader traces rays through a Signed Distance Field (SDF) consisting of a deformed sphere core, a tilted torus, and a satellite sphere. Lighting includes soft shadows, ambient occlusion, Fresnel highlights, and distance-based fog. Quality toggle adjusts raymarch step count; pause/reset control animation and camera. Resolution scales with device pixel ratio. No external libraries—pure WebGL2 and vanilla JavaScript.
