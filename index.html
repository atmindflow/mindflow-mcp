Technical notes: WebGL/Animation design

The hero uses a single Three.js scene with a custom ShaderMaterial torus-knot and a modest particle field:

  • Rim lighting (view-normal falloff), iridescent banding (sine over a stable field), and low-frequency value noise to avoid flat gradients.
  • Interactive pulse via a uniform; pointer-damped orbit to keep motion subtle and accessible.
  • Performance: DPR capped at 2, no post-processing, visibility/reduced-motion aware, GPU resources disposed on disconnect.

See main.js for extensive inline comments.
